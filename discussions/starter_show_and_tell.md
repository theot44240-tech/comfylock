# 🎨 Show your workflow locks!

Welcome to **Show and Tell** — the place to share what you've pinned with ComfyLock.

Drop a reply with:

- **What the workflow does** (a portrait pipeline? a video upscaler? a LoRA stack?)
- **Your `.lock`** (paste it, or link it — feel free to redact private URLs)
- **A result image/clip**, if you can share one
- **Anything that surprised you** about making it reproducible

Why share? Someone hitting "why does this look different on my machine?" can grab
your lock, run `comfy-lock unpack`, and reproduce your setup exactly. That's the
whole point. 🔒

> New here? `pip install "git+https://github.com/theot44240-tech/comfylock.git"`,
> then `comfy-lock pack your_workflow.flow.json -r /path/to/ComfyUI`.
